The Forms for Sora in Kingdom Hearts 2

Form Name Image Description
Valor Form Increases Sora's strength and allows him to use two Keyblades.
Wisdom Form Enhances Sora's magic abilities and allows him to shoot magic projectiles.
Master Form Combines the abilities of both Valor and Wisdom Forms, increasing Sora's agility and magic power.
Final Form The most powerful form, granting Sora the ability to fly and use powerful Keyblade attacks.
Anti Form A dark form that increases Sora's speed but limits his abilities, representing his struggle against darkness.
Limit Form A form that allows Sora to use abilities from the original Kingdom Hearts, focusing on teamwork with Donald and Goofy.

These forms are integral to Sora's character development and gameplay mechanics in Kingdom Hearts 2, each offering unique abilities and playstyles.
